The Best Movies According to Mohd Fazal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bajrangi Bhaijaan

Bajrangi Bhaijaan (2015) is an Indian drama film about Pavan, a devout man, who helps a mute Pakistani girl, Munni, find her way home, showcasing love's power across borders

Taare Zameen Par

Taare Zameen Par (2007) is an Indian drama film directed by Aamir Khan. It sensitively portrays a dyslexic child's journey to self-discovery and an art teacher's efforts to understand and support him.

PK

PK (2014) is an Indian satirical sci-fi comedy film directed by Rajkumar Hirani. A stranger (played by Aamir Khan) questions religious beliefs on Earth, delivering thought-provoking and humorous insights on society.
